My experience in E-nitiate camp!

E-nitiate camp is the first camp that I attended . It's such a great experience . Not only I met new amazing friends , but also I'm sharing my passion with the other 24 participants. The teachers,organizers and volunteers are doing all their best to help us in everything The classes are just perfect , they're not boring at all . This camp taught me a lot of things about computer science such as "Linux is not an operating system" . It's always great to learn new things that will help you . The activities are very cool. Even though I'm a little bit tired by the end of the day but it's okey since I develop my knowledge in that field . Everyone is trying to give us the maximum of information about CS .
